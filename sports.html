Sports Day-MJC



The annual sports day meet organised by Michael Job College of arts & science for women was held on 11/02/2023.The sports day meet was conducted successfully. The chief guest Dr.Saravana Prabha,Associate Professor.

The Spirit of Sportsmanship:

At the heart of every Sports Day is the spirit of sportsmanship. It's not just about winning; it's about participating, giving your best, and respecting your opponents. Our students embody these values, and they will showcase not only their athletic prowess but also their commitment to fair play.

Diverse Competitions:

From track and field events to team sports, Sports Day at Micheal job college is a melting pot of various competitions. Students have been training diligently, and the air is buzzing with anticipation. Whether it's the sprint on the track, the agility of the soccer field, or the precision of a relay race, every event promises nail-biting excitement.

Team Colors and Enthusiastic Cheers:

One of the hallmarks of Sports Day is the vibrant display of team colors. Students, teachers, and even parents come adorned in their team's hues, creating a visual spectacle that reflects the unity within our community. The sound of enthusiastic cheers and chants reverberates through the air, creating an electrifying atmosphere.

Beyond Athletics:

While sports take center stage, Sports Day atMicheal job college is a holistic celebration. Cultural performances add a touch of artistry to the event, showcasing the diverse talents of our students. Dance, music, and drama performances provide a well-rounded experience, emphasizing that excellence extends beyond the sports field.

Inclusivity and Parental Involvement:

Sports Day is an event that brings the entire school community together. It's not only about students but also about parents cheering from the sidelines. Family picnics, cheering competitions, and parent-student activities enhance the inclusivity of the day, reinforcing the idea that sports are a shared experience.

The overall trophy was won by the Akasha team and Runner up by the Tejas team. After the prize distribution the vote of thanks was conveyed to the crowd followed by the National Anthem. Under the guidance of Principal Dr. Shalini Packiam Kamala, Physical Director Ms. D. Dharanipriya and all the four team coordinators the event was a great success.
